## The Approach

Started at Eaton Saddle just before sunrise. The trail is well-maintained but gains elevation quickly through sparse chaparral.

## The Summit <span id="summit" />

At 6,161 feet the views are unreal on a clear day. You can see all the way to Catalina Island to the south and the high desert to the north.

## The Descent

Took the same route back down. Legs were feeling it by the end but totally worth it.
